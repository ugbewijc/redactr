REDACTR - Scramble some words from a body of text Content

SOURCE

Redact app help people to safely share content on social media by scrambling portions of the text they don’t want the world to read/see. A user will write/paste some content into a form field (Content to scarmble field) and then specify the words that must be scrambled (words to scramble). Note that the user can specify more than one word (separated by space) to be scrambled. user can also specify what to use in replacing the words so that the app can do the scrambling with what the user provides, be it asterisks, question marks, dashes, underscores, or even other words.

After clicking a “Redact Now” button, Redact app scans through the original text and replaces the specified words to be scrambled with asterisks (i.e ****)
